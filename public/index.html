Group Discussion Competition for Students24th March 2019

The eminent speaker from ACM India, Dr. Abhijat Vichare was invited as a guest for the event of group discussion and to judge the competition. The event was conducted among 25 students.

Read more There were three rounds, where the time distribution for each round was 5 min., 7 min and 10 min., respectively. The students were judged based on some of the parameters that included Language, Body language, Voice Tone and Pitch, Group behavior, Team Spirit. In the first round, there were six groups, among which four groups were shortlisted for round II. Then from round II, two groups were shortlisted for the final round.

Visit to Gunjavne Village near Rajgad Fort 24th September 2018

The first year students of M.E. Computer Engineering, MIT, Pune visited Gunjavane Village near Rajgad Fort. This was conducted as a study tour with the intention to study the village life.

Read more To nderstand the problems faced by the villagers. There were around 30 people in this study group, including Computer Engineering and Electronics Engineering students and their professors. 1. Preliminary findings: a. Population around 1400 villagers spread across 8 wadis. b. Majorly rice is grown along with pulses. Tractor is brought on rent from neighboring village. c. Solar lights installed on the streets. d. River water used in Monsoon, well water used in other seasons. e. School only till 7th standard after which students have to travel to neighboring village for further education. 2 working computers with basic software installed in school. f. Vaccination is regularly done. 2. Problems faced: a. Villagers are worried about snake bites. b. No clinic in the village or medical store in the village. They have to travel around 20 km to get medicine and basic treatment. c. Government medical van visits the village once in 15 days for checkup. Only nurse, no doctor is present in the van. d. Reconstruction of dam is a priority for the villagers. e. Bus facility scheduled twice a day, but not regular.
